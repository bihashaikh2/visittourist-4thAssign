About Pakistan.!!

Pakistan, officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a country in South Asia. It is the world's fifth-most populous country with a population exceeding 212.2 million. By area, it is the 33rd-largest country, spanning 881,913 square kilometres

Pakistan is Famous For!

Pakistan has so far been the most successful country in fighting WOT (the war on terror). Pakistan is world's only Muslim Nuclear Power. Pakistan has the eleventh-largest armed forces in the world. Pakistan's Tarbela Dam is the largest earth-filled dam in the world (and fifth largest by structural volume).
